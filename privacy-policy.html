Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Healthocare Private Limited ("HealthO Pro", "we", "us" or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our products and services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you interact with us:

  • Contact details you provide via our forms, such as name, email address, phone number and organization details.
  • Information about your enquiry, including the products or services you are interested in.
  • Technical information such as IP address, browser type and usage data collected automatically.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your enquiries, demo requests and support tickets.
  • To provide, operate, maintain and improve our products and services.
  • To send you relevant information about our solutions, with your consent where required.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures — including encryption, access controls and audit logging — to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure or destruction.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our website and services, strictly under confidentiality obligations, or where required by law.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us. We will respond in accordance with applicable data-protection laws.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience and analyze usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, please contact us at support@healtho.pro or call +91 97007 10055.
